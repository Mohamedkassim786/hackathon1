# AIMS - Academic Information Management System Knowledge Base

## Admissions and Eligibility
The Academic Information Management System (AIMS) oversees the admission process for all undergraduate and postgraduate programmes.
- **Eligibility Criteria (Undergraduate)**: Minimum 70% in high school (10+2) with core subjects related to the chosen field. For Engineering, Mathematics and Physics are mandatory.
- **Eligibility Criteria (Postgraduate)**: Minimum 60% in a relevant Bachelor's degree.
- **Application Process**: 
  1. Register on the official AIMS portal.
  2. Upload necessary documents (transcripts, ID proof).
  3. Pay the application fee ($50).
  4. Schedule an entrance interview/test if applicable.
- **Deadlines**: Fall intake (August) - May 31st; Spring intake (January) - October 15th.

## Programmes and Courses
AIMS manages a diverse range of programmes:
1. **B.Tech in Computer Science**: Focuses on AI, Cybersecurity, and Software Engineering. (4-year programme).
2. **B.Sc in Data Science**: Heavily focused on statistics and big data analytics. (3-year programme).
3. **MBA in Digital Marketing**: Prepares students for leadership in the digital economy. (2-year programme).
4. **B.Des in UI/UX Design**: Combines creative arts with technical usability. (4-year programme).

## Facilities and Infrastructure
- **Computing Labs**: High-performance workstations equipped with the latest software development tools.
- **Digital Library**: Access to over 50,000 journals and 1 million e-books, open 24/7.
- **Innovation Hub**: A dedicated space for student startups and research projects.
- **Sports Complex**: Includes an Olympic-sized swimming pool, indoor stadium, and gym.
- **Hostel Accommodation**: Modern, air-conditioned rooms with high-speed Wi-Fi.

## Academic Practices and Services
- **Mentorship Programme**: Every student is assigned a faculty mentor for academic and career guidance.
- **Credit System**: A flexible choice-based credit system (CBCS) allowing students to take interdisciplinary electives.
- **Student Portal**: A centralized system for tracking attendance, grades, and fee payments.

## Student Outcomes and Placements
- **Placement Cell**: Actively coordinates with top-tier companies for internships and full-time roles.
- **Top Recruiters**: Tech giants, consulting firms, and creative agencies.
- **Average Package**: $85,000 for Engineering graduates; $75,000 for Business graduates.
- **Alumni Network**: A global network of 10,000+ alumni providing networking opportunities.

## Appointment with Academic Counselor
To book a session with a counselor, please provide:
1. Student Name
2. Inquiry Type (Admission, Course Choice, Career Guidance)
3. Preferred Date and Time
Once provided, the AIMS assistant will coordinate with the academic office.
